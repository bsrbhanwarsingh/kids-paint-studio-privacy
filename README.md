# Privacy Policy for Kids Paint Studio

**Last Updated:** December 26, 2025

## Introduction

Kids Paint Studio is a children's painting and drawing application designed for kids to express their creativity. This privacy policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use.

## Information Collection and Use

**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

Kids Paint Studio is designed to work completely offline. All drawings and creations are stored locally on your device only.

### What We Don't Collect:
- ❌ No personal information (names, emails, etc.)
- ❌ No location data
- ❌ No usage analytics
- ❌ No advertising identifiers
- ❌ No cloud storage or syncing

### What Is Stored Locally:
- ✅ Drawings created within the app (stored only on your device)
- ✅ App settings and preferences (stored only on your device)
- ✅ Selected age group and child's first name (stored only on your device, never transmitted)

## Internet Permission

The app requires internet permission only for:
1. **App Updates**: Checking for and downloading app updates from our official source
2. **No other network activity**: We do not transmit any user data

## COPPA Compliance

Kids Paint Studio is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
- All app functionality works offline
- No third-party advertising or analytics
- No social sharing features

## GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union:
- No personal data is collected or processed
- All data remains on the user's device
- Parents have full control over all app data through device settings

## Parental Rights

Parents and guardians have the right to:
- Review any data stored on the device (accessible through device file manager)
- Delete all app data (through device settings > Apps > Kids Paint Studio > Clear Data)
- Uninstall the app at any time

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- We recommend using device-level security (screen lock, encryption)
- Regular backups of your device will preserve drawings
- Uninstalling the app will delete all drawings unless backed up

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new privacy policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
